\u6ce2 The Mission

Crypto markets are chaotic, beautiful, and alive. PumpRadar.space was built on a single premise: market data should feel as alive as the art it inspires.

We fuse real-time on-chain intelligence \u2014 pump signals, whale wallet movements, volume anomalies \u2014 into an aesthetic dashboard that doesn't just inform, it immerses.

We believe the next generation of crypto tools must go beyond raw numbers and speak to the human instinct for pattern, beauty, and meaning.

  • Real-time pump detection visualized as Ukiyo-e wave blooms
  • Whale wallet alerts displayed through Kintsugi gold crack animations
  • Murakami-inspired rainbow signals for dual pump+whale events
  • Kusama polka-dot patterns encoding volume density at a glance
  • Zero clutter \u2014 every pixel carries signal, not noise
  • Mobile-first, responsive art dashboard for any device

Visual Language Guide
\u8996\u899a\u8a00\u8a9e

\u8272 Color Meanings

  • Cyan / Teal \u2014 Pump signal active, upward price momentum detected
  • Gold / Kintsugi \u2014 Whale wallet activity, large holder accumulation
  • Sakura Pink \u2014 Negative price change, downward pressure
  • Violet / Rainbow \u2014 Dual signal: pump + whale converging
  • Grey / Muted \u2014 Token in quiet state, no active signals

\u52d5 Animation Meanings

  • Pulsing rings \u2014 Real-time pump momentum, rings = signal strength
  • Gold shimmer sweep \u2014 Active whale movement in the last 15 minutes
  • Kintsugi veins (hover) \u2014 Represents the beauty of market fracture and recovery
  • Polka dot density \u2014 Volume concentration (Kusama-inspired data encoding)
  • Kanji glyph scale \u2014 Hover growth = signal confidence score
